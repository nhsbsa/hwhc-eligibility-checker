How to get your help

In most cases, you don't have to do anything more, although you may have to show proof you get Pension Credit Guarantee Credit to your pharmacist, optician or dentist.

You can use your latest benefit letter or award notice as proof. If you have lost yours contact the Jobcentre Plus Helpline (opens in new tab).

Help with travel for NHS treatment

Tell the hospital (or the place where you're getting treatment) that you want to claim back the cost of your journey. They'll tell you what you need to do.

You may need to show proof:

  • of your travel costs, for example, bus tickets
  • that you're entitled to help

Warning

It's important you know what benefit you get - you can get a fine if you claim help with NHS costs and you're not entitled.
