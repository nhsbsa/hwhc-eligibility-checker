War pension exemption certificate

This certificate gives you help for treatment related to your accepted disablement, if you have an illness or injury caused by service in the armed forces.

What it includes

  • free NHS prescriptions
  • free NHS wigs and fabric supports

Important: Warning

Your certificate only covers prescription for your accepted disablement. If you're not sure, contact Veterans UK (opens in new tab) before you claim.

How to get your help

If you have an illness or injury caused by service in the armed forces, you need to contact Veterans UK (opens in new tab) to find out about the help you are entitled to.
