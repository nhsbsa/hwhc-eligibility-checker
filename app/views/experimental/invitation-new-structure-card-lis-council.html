NHS Low Income Scheme

Because you get help from your local council to pay for your care home, you may qualify for the NHS Low Income Scheme.

What it includes

You get free:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental check-ups and treatment
  • sight tests
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports

You get money off:

  • new glasses or contact lenses
  • repairing or replacing your glasses or contact lenses
  • travel for NHS treatment

How to get your help

  • You will need to complete an application.
  • You can do this online and it should take about 10 minutes.
Start an online application
Other ways to apply

You can apply by post using an HC1(SC) form.
