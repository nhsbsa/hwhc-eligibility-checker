War pension exemption certificate

If you have an illness or injury caused by service in the armed forces, this certificate gives you help for treatment related to your accepted disablement.

What it includes

Free

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports

Important: Warning

Your certificate only covers prescription for your accepted disablement. If you're not sure, contact Veterans UK before you claim.

How to get your help

If you have an illness or injury caused by service in the armed forces, you need to contact Veterans UK to find out about the help you are entitled to.

You may be asked to show your certificate at the pharmacy, clinic or hospital.
